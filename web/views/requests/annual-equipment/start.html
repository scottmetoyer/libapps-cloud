Annual Equipment Request
Annual Equipment Requests are used to submit requests for annual equipment and furniture purchases. These requests are reviewed, prioritized, and approved by the Library AULs and UL. You can check on the status of an existing requestby clicking Annual Equipment in the sidebar to the left.
